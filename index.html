I am a Master of Business Analytics student at the Sobey School of Business, Saint Mary's University in Halifax. My work bridges the gap between technical data science and real-world business strategy.

I hold degrees in Computer Engineering and International Business Management — a combination that lets me speak both the language of algorithms and the language of business. I began my analytics journey in Saskatoon before relocating to Halifax to deepen my expertise in applied analytics.

I work primarily with R and SQL, and apply Python for agentic AI and RAG projects. My passion is transforming complex, messy data into clear and actionable insights — and exploring the exciting frontier of Agentic AI.

Previously, I worked in marketing and data support — which is where my curiosity for analytics was first sparked. I enjoy tackling challenging problems and finding creative, data-driven solutions.
